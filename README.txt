Accessing Your Files: 
Insert your USB drive into your computer. 
Open your USB folder to find your web dev folder. 
Opening your Projects:
Double-click the .html file to launch your website in your browser.
Right-click and select “Open with” if you wish to choose a different browser.
Accessing your Github Repository:
Go to github.com and log into your account.
Your repository contains the same files as your USB.
If not: click add files and select the web dev folder from your USB. Finally, commit and push those changes.
